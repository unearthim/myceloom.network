The Core Concept

The protocol replaces centralized search algorithms with Semantic Resonance. It uses two simple HTML lines to define your node:

  1. The Spore Line (Identity): what you are (signals, lineage, vibe).
  2. The Mother Tree Line (Relationship): where you are rooted (origin, hub, inspiration).
